Go Red For Women

Watson will be participating in Wear Red Day on February 1, 2019. On this day, men and women are encouraged to wear red as a symbol of their support of women’s heart health.

If you would like to participate you can dothe following:

  • Wear Red on February 1, 2019
  • Make a donation to the American Heart Association.

Wear Red Day 2018

The following is information about last year's Wear Red Day.

One of Watson’s close to home charities is the American Heart Association. Chances are you, or a family member knows someone that is affected by heart disease and stroke.
Looking at the statistics,

  • 2200 Americans die of cardiovascular disease each day, this breaks down to one death every forty seconds.
  • Cardiovascular diseases, which include stokes, claim the lives of about one woman every 80 seconds.

to get a little ahead of the game we started a raffle on Friday, January 19th, 2018 to run all the way to Go Red For Women day on February 2nd! Watson intends to match the money raised from the raffle and donate it to The American Heart Association. The total amount raised by seventy-three Watson employees by the raffle was $559. Watson as a company matched the total rose by its employees which brought us to a total donation of $1,118.00.

A letter from Jim Watson, President Watson Inc.

July 26, 2017

Dear Colleague,

I hope that you and your family are enjoying your summer. Though a portion of this letter is directed to employees who were hired prior to June 1, 2017, it addresses all employees. If you have any questions regarding any of this information, I encourage you to speak to your Supervisor, Human Resources, me or anyone on the Leadership Team.

Last year we faced a lot of change and transition that brought us many challenges, some of which we are still facing today. Our Rallying Cry of On Time Delivery and Right First Time is still our most important goal and focusing on this goal is critical to our success this year.

We will win if we remember we belong to one team like the Yankees. Everyone's role is important. No one's role in the company is more important than another. By making the commitment to encourage, help each other and work together as a team we can overcome these challenges. If we keep this as our focus, I am very optimistic about Watson's performance for the remainder of the year.

We had an excellent first half with record sales and profits. We would like to thank those employees, who were hired prior to June 1st 2017, with the exception of the Leadership Team, with a $500.00 bonus that you will receive in your July 28th paycheck. This is in addition to the end of the year Profit Sharing Plan that employees participate in. You have all made important contributions to our success and this is a way of expressing our thanks.

Thank you for your support it gives me pleasure each day to come in and work with you as part of our team.

Sincerely,

Jim Watson
President
